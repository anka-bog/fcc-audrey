Audrey Hepburn

Elegance is the only beauty that never fades

Audrey Hepburn, always classy and charming.

Timeline:

  • 1929 - born in Ixelles, a munipality in Brussels, Belgium
  • 1948 - film debut as an air stewardess in Dutch in Seven Lessons
  • 1951 - theatre debut in the Broadway play Gigi in a title role
  • 1953 - first starring role in Roman Holiday as Princess Ann
  • 1953 - Golden Globe Award for Best Actress, Academy Award for Best Actress and BAFTA Award for Best British Actress in a Leading Role
  • 1954 - marriage with Mel Ferrer
  • 1955 - first misscarriage
  • 1960 - birth of her son Sean Hepburn Ferrer
  • 1968 - divorce with Mel Ferrer
  • 1969 - marriage with Andrea Dotti
  • 1970 - birth of her second son Luca Dotti
  • 1980 - beginning of the relationship with Robert Wolders
  • 1982 - divorce with Andrea Dotti
  • 1988 - mission in Ethiopia
  • 1990 - work for UNICEF in Vietnam
  • 1992 - mission in Somalia
  • 1993 - died in her sleep at home in Tolochenaz
